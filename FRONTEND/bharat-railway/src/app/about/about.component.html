About Us

Bharat Railway Corporation Ltd. (BRC) is a “Mini Ratna (Category-I)” Central Public Sector Enterprise under Ministry of Railways, Government of India. BRC was incorporated on 10th December, 2019 as an extended arm of the Indian Railways to upgrade, professionalize and manage the catering and hospitality services at stations, on trains and other locations and to promote domestic and international tourism through development of free wi-fi, special tour packages, information & commercial publicity and global reservation systems. The authorized capital of the company is 50 crores and paid up capital is 20 crores, fully subscribed by Ministry of Railways, Government of India. It’s registered and Corporate Office is situated at New Delhi.

The core activities of the Company are detailed below:


  • Catering & Hospitality
  • Internet Ticketing
  • Travel & Tourism
  • Packaged Drinking Water

At present, the Company operates through:


  • Five Zonal Offices at New Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata, Chennai & Secundrabad
  • Five base kitchens located at New Delhi, Howrah, Ahmedabad, Patna, Mumbai Central
  • One Internet Ticketing Office at New Delhi
